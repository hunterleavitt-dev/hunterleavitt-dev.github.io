Hello and welcome to my website. You may find yourself wondering about the design, how everything from the font to the gradients on buttons look as though they have been kidnapped from a 2008 instance of Windows Vista; however, these design choices stem from a pattern known as Skeuomorphism.

Furthermore, the color choices, high visual reflectivity and semiotics conveyed through humble icons mirror that which would have been seen in many software systems circa ~2002-2011. This design trend is known retroactively as Frutiger Aero, and I hope you enjoy these digital artifacts during your time on the site.
